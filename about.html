Welcome to Frieda & Sophie

We’re a mother–daughter duo based in Norway, creating jewellery boxes that hold more than just your treasures — they carry stories, memories, and meaning.

Each piece is thoughtfully curated, designed, and handcrafted using embroidered mulberry silk and other beautiful materials. With a blend of traditional craftsmanship and everyday elegance, our boxes are perfect for gifting or keeping your most cherished pieces safe.

Rooted in love, simplicity, and connection — our collection celebrates the little things worth holding onto.

Thank you for being here.
— Frieda & Sophie
